Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Trust

Founded in 2014 in Indore, HopeRoot began with a single classroom and twelve children. Today we work across three districts, partnering with local governments, schools, and health workers to create lasting change — not charity.

  • 01
    Community First
    Every program is designed with, not for, the communities we serve.
  • 02
    Accountability
    We publish annual impact reports and spend at least 88 paise of every rupee on programs.
  • 03
    Long-term Thinking
    We invest in skills and systems, not short-term relief, for sustainable futures.
What We Do

Our Core Programs

Partner With Us →

Education Access

Free tutoring centres, digital literacy, and scholarship support for students in Classes 1–12 across rural Madhya Pradesh.

Community Health

Monthly health camps, maternal care support, and training local health volunteers to bridge the gap between clinics and homes.

Women's Livelihoods

Skill development workshops, SHG formation, and microfinance linkages empowering 2,000+ women with sustainable incomes.

Shelter & WASH

Clean drinking water, sanitation infrastructure, and temporary shelter support for disaster-affected families.

Youth Leadership

After-school clubs, debate & sports, and a flagship Young Changemakers Programme for teenagers aged 14–18.

Volunteer Network

A 400-member volunteer corps of professionals, students, and retirees powering every program on the ground.
